A theological and apologetics resource for the seeker, the new believer, and the believer who wants to grow deeper.

David faced Goliath with nothing but a sling, five stones, and an unshakable confidence that the battle belonged to the Lord. This site exists for the same kind of fight — for clear, doctrinally grounded answers about who God is, what the Gospel actually says, and how to live out the Christian life with conviction.
